So, what issue are we

solving?

A lack of Manpower

Many organizations lack the amount of staff or volunteers willing to/capable of finding the most efficient routes to their desired destinations. This app will not require any manual work, being fully automatic, and can be directly handled by the drivers. This makes the organization's responisbilites significantly less burdensome and makes the process more efficient too.

Efficiency and Speed

As of right now, randomized routes are extremely inefficient and can actually take more time and energy than necessary. Our app allows users to optimize their routes efficiently so that locations are approached in a logical and straightforwards manner with no time or resource on the road is wasted. It's crucial to provide the shortest route for our drivers to reach a specific number of homes efficiently, ensuring timely deliveries for kids in need.

Variable Recipient Status

Since services nowadays count a lot on the recipient; where they are, are they sick, have they moved locations?, this service allows a flexible and easily interchangeable route finder. Our app reduces the concern for uncertainity that organizations may worry about, and increases the stability of their prgrams.

here are our product

Features

Time Slot Scheduling and Vehicle Specification

When the driver starts a new delivery, they are to choose a time slot given in the app that allow organizations to have organized deliveries. This lets users organize their efforts more clearly and give suppliers time to restock in between.

This feature is functional for organizations such as Food4kids that need to make sure drivers can carry their loads and that they are still in stock. These features are shown if the user logs in with a food4kids or related organization domain! (similar to Uwaterloo learn)

Driver Profile, Saved Locations, and Routes

Our app allows the user to have a profile with their saved locations, routes, preferences, and more. This allows users like Food4talk to reuse their old routes and easily edit them to accomodate for any changes in their program. Organizations can have multiple driver profiles that they monitor and assign routes to.

This also makes seeing past locations easy, such that any old information is recoverable. Users don't need to worry about having to reinput data or starting over ever. *note: this is an example on google maps of what saved locations could look like.

Optimized Route Calculation

Our app optimizes the routes between locations/addresses using C++ that treats the city like a grid and calculates the shortest possible distance between each chosen destination, taking into consideration the streets of the city and the constraints of the vehicle.

You can input any number of addresses and the app, using google maps, will output a route for the user to follow.
